MARS

Mars, the fourth planet from the Sun, is often called the "Red Planet" due to its iron oxide-rich surface, giving it a reddish appearance. It has a thin atmosphere primarily composed of carbon dioxide, with temperatures averaging around -80 degrees Fahrenheit. Known for its vast volcanoes, deep canyons, and evidence of ancient water flows, Mars is a key focus for exploration. Scientists study it for potential signs of past life and future human colonization.
